Who We Are

Mil‐Spec Restorations is a team of restoration experts dedicated to the preservation of military history

Collectors ranging from individuals to museums trust us because we deliver world class restoration every time.

Our experienced, professional, highly skilled craftsmen are second to none and our large well‐equipped workshop allows us to take on projects big and small, from Land Rovers to tanks, limbers to 25pdr field guns and much more

Services

Restoration World class restoration services.

Mil‐Spec Restorations delivers world class restorations for collectors large and small. With our experienced, professional, highly skilled craftsmen and large well‐equipped workshop, we can take on, and have done, almost any sized project from tanks to Land Rovers. As all military vehicle collectors know there are different levels of restoration and without question, when it comes time to sell your vehicle, the best restorations achieve the highest prices.

Parts store World class restoration services.

We offer for sale some of the rare parts we have fabricated to complete past projects. If we needed  them, you might too.

Reverse Engineering and fabrication

With any restoration there are components that are simply unobtainable and must be reverse engineered and fabricated. We have a great deal of experience in this area and with our 4 axis CNC milling centre and CNC lathe there isn’t much we can’t fabricate, we made over 300 separate parts for the M41 Walker Bulldog restoration alone.

Spin on oil filter housing for Morris
Minor, low light, side valve, series MM engines
Part No: 100OFH001 Manufacturer: Mil-Spec Restorations
Fits:
Make: Morris Engine Model: Morris Minor, low light, side valve, series MM
Year: 1948-1950 Oil filter: R386
Description:

This spin on oil filter housing is a replacement for the standard cartridge housing fitted to these engines. It takes a common R386 paper element spin on oil filter making changing the filter a breeze, avoiding the mess associated with cartridge filters.

CNC machined from solid aluminium bar stock with a threaded steel insert for mounting the oil filter.
